Privacy Policy

Your data, not ours

Effective date: 5 May 2026

Glow Moon does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your Mac — no analytics, no tracking, no third-party services, no network calls.

What we collect

Nothing.

This Period Tracker app is designed with privacy in mind. All data you enter, such as cycle information, dates, and preferences, is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or health-related data to external servers.

Third-party services

None.

The app contains no analytics SDKs, advertising libraries, crash reporters, or any other third-party tracking tools.

Network access

The app does not use the network.

This Period Tracker app operates entirely offline. All calculations, predictions, and data processing (such as cycle tracking and reminders)are performed locally on your device.

Cookies and tracking

We do not track your activity or collect any usage data. All information you enter (such as cycle data and preferences) is stored locally on your device using standard Apple frameworks like UserDefaults

Children's privacy

This Period Tracker app is designed to respect user privacy for all users. The app does not knowingly collect, store, or share any personal information, including from children.

Your rights

Privacy regulations such as the GDPR and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grant you rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal data held about you. Because we do not hold any data about you, there is nothing to access, correct, delete, or export. Uninstalling the app removes any locally stored preferences from your device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is ever updated, the revised version will be posted at this same URL with a new effective date. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.
